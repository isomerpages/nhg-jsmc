<p>Courtyard Singapore Novena is conveniently located in Singapore's city
center, adjacent to the Novena MRT/subway station. This Novena hotel is
10 minutes by subway to the Orchard Road shopping belt and 15 minutes by
car to the Central Business District or Gardens by the Bay. Singapore National
Stadium and Singapore Indoor Stadium are just 6 km away.Enjoy contemporary
rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows offering panoramic cityscape views.
Unwind at the rooftop infinity pool or work out at the 24-hour gym. Enjoy
quality meals at the all-day dining restaurant, Sky22, then follow your
feast with craft cocktails at Las Palmas rooftop poolside bar. Alternatively,
a variety of dining options are also available at the three shopping malls
seamlessly connected to our Singapore city center hotel. For a private
business meeting or celebration, opt for the hotel's modern function room
with an adjoining patio or Las Palmas' intimate private function room offering
breathtaking views of the CBD area.</p>
